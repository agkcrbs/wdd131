Family Website Site Plan

1. Site Name

Site Name: Our Foster Family

This is a personal, family-focused website meant to share updates, interests, and connections.

Optional Domain Availability: ourfosters.family (not verified)

2. Site Purpose

This is a central space for our family to share updates, projects, and interests, and to practice web development together. Each person will have their own page to introduce themselves and share content. Later, message posting may be added.

3. Scenarios

  • How can I view each person page and find out about them?
  • Can I contribute to the site myself?

4. Color Schema

  • #A2DAFF – Light blue: Used for background color.
  • #006EB6 – Deep blue: Used for headings and accents.
  • #292929 – Charcoal black: Used for navigation elements.

5. Typography

  • "Outfit", Trebuchet, sans-serif – Used for main headings and body text.
  • "Dancing Script", sans-serif – Used for special headings.
  • "Times New Roman", serif – Used for navigation.
